Mykhailo Mudryk

Mykhailo Petrovych Mudryk is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Premier League club Chelsea and the Ukraine national team.

  • Mudryk is a right-footed winger who is most comfortable cutting in from the left onto his stronger foot
  • His acceleration is a particular strength, as he is able to reach his top speed extremely quickly from a standing start.

02

Joao Felix

João Félix Sequeira is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Chelsea, on loan from Atlético Madrid of La Liga, and the Portugal national team

  • He has been deployed as a striker, as a second striker, or even as a winger, although his primary position is that of an attacking midfielder.
  • Félix is regarded as a highly skilful and technical player

03

Malo Gusto

Malo Gusto (born 19 May 2003) is a French professional footballer who plays as a right-back for Ligue 1 club Lyon, on loan from Premier League club Chelsea.

  • Malo Gusto is seen as a very agile and offensively minded player, ever since playing eight-a-side football at Villefontaine, and he first played as an attacking midfielder or a forward.
  • He is seen as both a very athletic and hard working fullback, who uses his technique and precision of pass to be decisive in the opponent's half.
